Project Overview

Tariq Al-Najah DZ is an AI-powered digital platform designed to centralize educational and professional opportunities for Algerian youth.

The challenge

Students in Algeria face fragmented and unreliable access to scholarships, internships, competitions, and career opportunities. Information is scattered across multiple platforms, leading to missed deadlines, limited mentorship, and weak alignment between student skills and market demands.

The solution

Tariq Al-Najah DZ is an AI-powered digital platform that centralizes verified opportunities and delivers personalized recommendations based on user profiles. It connects students with relevant academic and professional pathways while providing structured tracking and support tools.

Key Technical Features

AI-Based Opportunity Matching
Centralized Opportunity Hub
User Dashboard
CRM & Partnership System
