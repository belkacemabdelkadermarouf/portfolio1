Tariq Al-najah

View Project Repository

Project Overview

Tariq Al-Najah bridges digital technology and physical Youth Houses to create a unified national ecosystem that connects Algerian youth to opportunities, skills, and civic engagement when it matters most. It transforms fragmented information into a structured, accessible, and actionable pathway for growth.

The challenge

Algerian youth face a fragmented ecosystem where opportunities, guidance, and collaboration are scattered and difficult to access, causing missed chances and limiting their full potential.

The solution

We developed Tariq Al-Najah, an integrated national digital ecosystem that centralizes and maps opportunities across Algeria, connects youth through intelligent community matching, provides structured skill-development pathways, and integrates Youth Houses as physical activation hubs—transforming fragmented information into a unified, accessible, and action-driven network that empowers every young Algerian to discover, pursue, and achieve their full potential.

Key Technical Features

Smart Opportunity Finder
Youth House Integration Module
National Synergy Architecture
Skill & Badge System
